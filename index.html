If you're really struggling

This app is for support and awareness, not emergency care. If you ever feel like you might hurt yourself, or you feel completely overwhelmed:

  • Talk to someone you trust, right away.
  • Reach out to a mental health professional or your doctor.
  • If you're in the United States, you can call or text 988 for 24/7 support.
  • In any country, you can contact your local emergency number if you are in immediate danger.
